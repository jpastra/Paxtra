Business Analyst

I've seen many con confusion regarding these two positions: Business Analyst and System Analyst.

It seems for some or many that these two positions do the same activities or same tasks but with a different name, but, these positions are different and performe different tasks. Honestly I have the same doubt and I took a course to know which is the difference between these two positions and understand better my activities as Business Analyst.

I'm going to share the following grid to understand the activities of the following positions:

The pointed that all written before is about roles. The rol that I've been performance is most of the time deppending of the company and project. In some companies have the positions and roles, but in others not. And all is related to the size of the project. Small project only can be handled by one role or a Business Analyst, but in long projects is completely necessary taking in cosideration a Project Manager, a Business Analyst and a System Analyst.

All adds up to all depends of the company and what kind of tasks/activities the position is going to perform and according to all these the positions that I have been working on have been as: Business Analyst and System Analyst. And YES I've worked as BA and SA in National and International Companies.
